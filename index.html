ABOUT THE ARCHIVE
This soundscape map is a collaborative archive for field recordings made during the **Félix Blume Field Recording Workshop** in Azillanet, France.

We collect sound notes captured from the historic town center, paths of the dry plateau, and hidden bushy gorges to visualize them on an acoustic map.

Use SoundCloud, Freesound, or direct upload methods to store your recordings without storage limits, and easily share sound data with colleagues using simple export/import text strings.
